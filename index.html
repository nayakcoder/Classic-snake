How to Play

Use the arrow keys to control the snake.

Eat the food to grow longer and earn points.

Avoid hitting the walls, obstacles, or yourself!

Collect power-ups and use special abilities to help you survive.

Abilities:

  • Phase Dash [SPACE]: Temporarily phase through obstacles and yourself
  • Time Warp [SHIFT]: Slow down time briefly
  • Magnet [CTRL]: Pull all food and power-ups toward you

Biomes:

  • Fire Biome: Increases speed but gives more points
  • Ice Biome: Slows turning but reduces obstacle count
  • Magnetic Biome: Attracts food but also attracts obstacles
  • Toxic Biome: Periodically spawns toxic clouds but special food gives extra abilities

Power-ups:

  • Speed Boost: Temporarily increases your speed
  • Shield: Protects from one collision
  • Food Magnet: Attracts food items
  • Bomb: Clears all obstacles
  • Star Power: Temporary invincibility

Game Modes:

  • Classic: Traditional snake gameplay with modern features
  • Time Attack: Score as many points as possible in 2 minutes
  • Survival: Obstacles increase over time, survive as long as possible
  • Zen: No game over, just relaxing snake gameplay
